This is where I'm writing down what I'm learning as I work with AI tools, models, and the workflows that wrap around them. It's part second brain, part public notes, part changelog. The stuff I'd otherwise lose in a Notes.app graveyard lives here instead, where future-me (and maybe you) can actually find it.

I've been using coding agents daily for a while now, and most of what I've figured out has come from trial, error, and paying attention to what actually ships. I don't have a grand thesis about any of it. I just want a place to think out loud without the shape of a Twitter thread or a tidy blog post getting in the way.

Some of what ends up here will be rough. A note about a keybinding, a half-formed opinion about evals, a fragment that might become a longer piece later. Other things will be more polished — guides I've tested on myself, case studies of projects I actually shipped. I'll mark each entry with a maturity level so you know what you're reading.

If something here is useful to you, that's a bonus. Mostly I'm writing it for me.
